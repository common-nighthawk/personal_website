About

Daniel Deutsch is currently a student at Dev Bootcamp in Chicago. He hopes to become a full stack developer and will be looking for a job in web/software development upon completion of the program.

Daniel spent the prior ~2 years working for NOOK Media (a subsidiary of/the digital business for Barnes & Noble) in the Digital Newsstand division. As the Senior Marketing Analyst he created, executed, and analyzed consumer campaigns across multiple channels including online, email, on-device, social media, and in-store for new customer aquisition and subscriber retention. Before that Daniel spent ~2 years at Time Inc. in the Consumer Marketing department for the News titles--TIME, Fortune, and Money magazines--focusing on retention and billing. Daniel was part of the team that launched Time Inc.'s first major project in the digital space, TIME All Acceess.

Daniel graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and minors in Mathematics, Mechanical Engineering, and English. He spent the better part of a year traveling through South America, the Middle East, and Europe. His interests include reading, running, and all things Brooklyn--where he currently resides.
